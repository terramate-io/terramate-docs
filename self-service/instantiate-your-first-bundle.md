# Instantiate Your First Bundle

## Prerequisites

- [Terramate CLI installed](/cli/installation)
- At least one available bundle

## Steps

1) Scaffold:

```sh
terramate scaffold
```

2) Generate:

```sh
terramate generate
```

3) Verify generated stack files and run your normal plan.
